This is Me – My Journey in Phases

From unsure beginnings to growing confidence and future ambition, this is a simple portfolio that tells the story of my personal and creative journey.

I Used to Be an Average Person With No Skill

At the beginning, I didn’t know what I was good at. I had no specific talent or direction. Like many people, I was just going with the flow, unsure of what the future held. But I always knew I didn’t want to remain average.

Now I'm a Wig Stylist and a Graphic Designer

I’ve discovered my creativity. Today, I design beautiful graphics for flyers, posts, and more. I also make and style wigs professionally. These skills have opened up new doors for me and helped me gain confidence and income.

In the Future, I Plan to Upskill

I'm aiming to learn UI/UX design and cloud computing to expand my knowledge and opportunities. My goal is to combine creativity and tech to solve real problems and build something meaningful.
